About Me

Welcome!
I am Vojislav and this is my portfolio website.

I live and work in Serbia. I got a degree in Astrophysics at the Belgrade university, and I am currently working as a physics teacher in school.

I love physics, astrophysics and math. But I also love web technologies and programming. And I have decided to pursue a new career in web development. This website is one of the many projects that I have done during my web development education at Bov Academy.
The most interesting fact about this website is that it is completely built by using only HTML and CSS. There is no JavaScript involved in creation of this website.

I am a person that has many interests and hobbies. As a teenager I was playing drums in a band, and I also play a guitar at the basic level. During my teenage years and as a young adolescent I was practicing skydiving which was one of the hobbies that I was most passionate about. Later I started to ride a mountain bike and explore the surroundings of my town and further areas. That lead to a 20 day bicycle road trip to Greece, a non-forgetting experience.

I also like hiking, scuba diving, building stuff, making experiments with various things, and together with my brother, I am slowly learning to work with honey bees.

Check out the recumbent bike that I have built with the help of my brother, and these Camera Obscura images that I made.

The mountain on the image in the background is called "Suva planina" which literally means "Dry mountain", but the real meaning is "Bare mountain" because of the lack of trees on it's western side. My hometown is about 30km to the southwest of this mountain.
